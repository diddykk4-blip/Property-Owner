How would you like to repay?

Select your preferred method for retiring the market warrants used to complete your building. You can discuss this further with your assigned agent.

ℹ️

Your selection here is a preference, not a binding agreement. Final terms will be agreed upon with your WealthMarket agent after the property assessment.

🏘️

Option A — Rental Recovery

SELECTED

WealthMarket puts your building up for rent. All rental income goes to us until the full warrant amount plus interest is recovered. You regain full income rights after recovery.

Best for: Owners who want passive recovery Timeline: Typically 3–7 years
✂️

Option B — Property Partition

SELECTED

Your building is divided into two units. You keep one unit entirely — WealthMarket sells the other unit to recover the warrants. Split ratio is determined by the valuer.

Best for: Multi-unit buildings Timeline: Recovery on sale
🏦

Option C — Mortgage Onlending

SELECTED

A WealthMarket mortgage onlender pays for your building completion. You repay them directly with interest. You retain full ownership from day one. Onlenders receive special 10× reflation terms.

Best for: Owners wanting full title retention Timeline: Depends on mortgage term
⚠️

Not sure which to pick? Leave it as your best guess for now — your assigned WealthMarket agent will walk you through the implications before you sign any agreement.
